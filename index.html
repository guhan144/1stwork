Cyber Safety is trying to be safe on the internet and is the act of maximizing an user's awareness of personal safety and security risks to private information and property associated with using the internet, and the self-protection from cyber crime.

  1. Keep Personal Information Professional and Limited
  2. Keep Your Privacy Settings On
  3. Practice Safe Browsing
  4. Make Sure Your Internet Connection is Secure. Use a Secure VPN Connection
  5. Be Careful What You Download
  6. Choose Strong Passwords
  7. Make Online Purchases From Secure Sites
  8. Be Careful What You Post
  9. Be Careful Who You Meet Online
  10. Keep Your Antivirus Program Up To Date
